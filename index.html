About Fasal Rakshak

Fasal Rakshak is India's first decentralized parametric insurance platform designed specifically for farmers. Using blockchain technology and smart contracts, we provide instant, transparent payouts when weather conditions trigger crop losses.

Traditional crop insurance involves lengthy claim processes, paperwork, and delays. Our platform eliminates these barriers by automatically triggering payouts based on verified weather data, ensuring farmers receive support exactly when they need it.

Built by farmers, for farmers - we understand the challenges of Indian agriculture and are committed to creating a more resilient farming community.

How It Works

1

Register Your Farm

Provide basic details about your farm location, crop type, and farming area. Our system verifies your information using satellite data.

2

Weather Monitoring

Our smart contracts continuously monitor weather data from multiple sources including satellites, weather stations, and IoT sensors.

3

Smart Contract Execution

When weather conditions meet the trigger parameters (drought, excessive rain, etc.), smart contracts automatically execute payouts.

4

Instant Payout

Receive compensation directly to your digital wallet or bank account within 24 hours. No paperwork, no waiting periods.

Insurance Contracts

Drought Protection

Automatic payouts when rainfall drops below critical levels for your crop type and region.

  • Coverage: Up to ₹50,000 per acre
  • Trigger: 30% below normal rainfall
  • Premium: 3-5% of sum insured
  • Payout: Within 24 hours

Excess Rainfall Protection

Protection against crop damage due to excessive rainfall and flooding conditions.

  • Coverage: Up to ₹40,000 per acre
  • Trigger: 150% above normal rainfall
  • Premium: 2-4% of sum insured
  • Payout: Within 24 hours

Temperature Stress Protection

Coverage for crop losses due to extreme temperature variations during critical growth periods.

  • Coverage: Up to ₹35,000 per acre
  • Trigger: Temperature beyond crop tolerance
  • Premium: 2-3% of sum insured
  • Payout: Within 24 hours
